About LACBEE

The Liberal Arts College Behavioral and Experimental Economics (LACBEE) workshop brings together faculty working in Behavioral and Experimental Economics at Liberal Arts Colleges. This gathering establishes research and mentoring networks while providing opportunities to discuss the teaching of experimental and behavioral topics.

This fifth in-person workshop will feature both research presentations and mentoring discussions. We especially encourage participants to bring early-to-mid-stage work that would benefit most from collaborative input. For example, this is an ideal opportunity to receive focused feedback on an experimental design before beginning your sessions.

Previous workshops were hosted by Colorado College (2019, 2024), Carleton College (2022), and Haverford and Swarthmore Colleges (2023).

There is no registration fee for the workshop. Light breakfasts, coffee, lunches, and dinners will be provided. Participants should expect to pay for their own lodging and transportation.

To register as a presenter or attendee, please complete this form by Wednesday, April 2nd. If you wish to present, you'll be prompted to submit your paper title and abstract during registration. A tentative schedule will be distributed approximately one week later (April 9th).
